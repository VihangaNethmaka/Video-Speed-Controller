Extension Philosophy

Quick Speed Controller was built with two primary goals: simplicity and zero friction. We believe that controlling media playback should be as instant as hitting the play/pause button.

Unlike complex media players, this extension focuses only on speed, delivering the fastest, most reliable way to adjust your playback for everything from educational lectures to casual viewing.
